## Introduction

Gangan is a music platform where artists set their own subscription tiers and fans subscribe directly. No algorithm deciding who gets heard. No middleman taking a cut. The brand needed to communicate this instantly — not through copy, but through the mark itself.

The logo is a custom wordmark built from geometric precision. The double "g" creates a natural symmetry — two mirrored forms suggesting the artist and the fan, the two halves of the platform. Angular cuts paired with rounded terminals balance technical precision with musical warmth. No off-the-shelf typeface. Every letterform was drawn.

---

## Challenge

The primary challenge was to visually define a platform that rejects everything about the streaming industry. Spotify is green, algorithmic, corporate. Apple Music is red, curated, walled-garden. Gangan needed an identity that felt independent, direct, and unmistakably music-first — without looking like a startup that tried too hard.

The brand had to work across four platforms (web, iOS, Android, marketing site) and two completely different audiences: artists running a business and fans discovering music. Same mark. Different contexts. No confusion.

The identity also needed to signal its origin. Gangan is operated out of Lagos, Nigeria, built for the global independent music scene. Not "African music app." A music app, made in Africa.

## Solution: Logo Design

![Gangan wordmark — full configuration](/gangan-logo-full.png)

The Gangan logo is a custom wordmark. The defining feature is the double "g" — two mirrored forms that share the same geometric DNA. The first "g" leads. The second "g" echoes. Together they suggest the artist and the fan, the two halves of the platform, locked in symmetry.

The letterforms are drawn with angular outer cuts and rounded inner terminals. The angularity gives the mark structure and confidence. The rounding keeps it from feeling cold or corporate. This tension — precision and warmth, geometry and humanity — is the visual thesis of the entire brand.

![Gangan logo — horizontal lockup](/gangan-logo-horizontal.png)

The logo ships in three configurations:

- **Full wordmark** — for website headers, hero sections, and primary brand placement
- **Horizontal lockup** — for tight spaces, navigation bars, and co-branded contexts
- **Standalone symbol** — the double-g mark extracted for app icons, favicons, and social avatars

![Gangan symbol — standalone double-g mark](/gangan-logo-symbol.png)

Each configuration was tested at app-icon scale (64×64px), website-header scale, and social-template scale. The symbol remains legible at 16×16px. The wordmark holds its character down to 80px wide.

## Solution: Color Palette

![Gangan brand color palette](/gangan-brand-colors.png)

The palette is anchored in deep indigo — nearly black, but with a blue undertone that reads as intentional rather than default. This dark foundation does two things: it differentiates Gangan from the bright, saturated palettes of streaming incumbents, and it puts album art and artist photography center stage. The music provides the color.

Accent colors are used sparingly. Vibrant orange for primary actions and brand moments. A cooler teal for secondary interactions and data visualization. The contrast ratio between the indigo surface and white text exceeds WCAG AAA (7:1 minimum).

The palette is designed for dark-mode-first interfaces. Light-mode variants invert the hierarchy: warm off-white surfaces with the indigo used for text and UI chrome.

---

## Solution: Typography

The wordmark uses custom letterforms — not a modified existing typeface, but drawn from geometric primitives. Every curve was adjusted optically. The "a" is single-story for a contemporary feel. The "n" repeats the same counter-width to create internal rhythm across the word.

For the product UI, the system uses a single sans-serif type family across all four platforms. One weight scale. One line-height rhythm. The reading experience for lyrics, artist bios, and tier descriptions is consistent from a 5-inch phone screen to a 27-inch desktop monitor.

## Solution: Applications

### App store assets

![Gangan app store screenshots](/gangan-appstore.png)

App store screenshots were designed to communicate the product's core value in a single scroll: artists get paid directly, fans get early access, no algorithm in between. Each screenshot pairs a feature (tiers, earnings, library, player) with a one-line value proposition. The dark background makes the UI screens pop against the App Store and Play Store's white browsing contexts.

### Social templates

![Gangan social media template — day one launch](/gangan-social-1.png)

Launch-day social templates extend the brand language beyond the product. The double-g symbol anchors every template. Typography follows the same geometric principles as the wordmark. The dark indigo background with orange accent creates instant brand recognition in a feed dominated by bright, noisy content.

![Gangan social media template — early access](/gangan-social-2.png)

The social system was designed to be modular — any team member can swap the headline, body text, and background asset without breaking brand consistency. The template does the heavy lifting.

### Website

The marketing site (gangan.app) uses the full wordmark as its primary brand signal. The dark indigo background fills the viewport. Scrolling sections alternate between brand messaging and product screenshots. The identity system — logo, colors, typography, spacing — propagates from Figma to Webflow with zero translation loss.

---

## Result

Gangan launched in May 2026 with a cohesive visual identity across all four platforms and marketing surfaces. The logo appears on the App Store and Google Play Store, the marketing website, social media, and inside the product itself — as an app icon, a loading screen, and a navigation brand mark.

The identity does what it was designed to do: it signals "different" at a glance. No green. No red. No algorithm. Just the mark.
